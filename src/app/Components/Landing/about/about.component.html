About us

We are located in Kitui county, Mutonguni ward near Ivulyamuu Market and along River Kithyoko.

We deal with production of farm product in large scale. Some of our main products are: onions, sukuma wiki, water melons, tomatoes, green maize, and pawpaws

You can contact us for free retails delivies in Kalambya, Muthioni, Kivani, and Matinga markets. Free deliveries also available for whole sale in Kabati, Kithyoko, Kakeani, Tulia, and Katutu markets
